# Privacy Policy — Octano

**Last updated: July 5, 2026**

Octano is a free iOS app, maintained by an independent developer, that helps drivers choose where to fuel up using official public data. This policy explains, in accordance with Brazil's General Data Protection Law (Law No. 13,709/2018 — LGPD), what data is processed and how.

**Controller:** Luis Fontinelles — contact: **[fontinelles.com/contact](https://fontinelles.com/contact)**

## The honest summary

Octano has **no account, no login, no advertising and does not sell data**. The content you create (prices, votes) stays on your device. Two things leave the device: your **approximate location**, sent to Google to find nearby stations, and **anonymous usage data**, sent to Firebase (Google) so we can understand how the app is used and improve it. Optional support purchases are processed by **Apple** — we never see your card data.

## 1. Data the app processes

### 1.1 Location (with your permission)
- **Why:** to find nearby stations, sort the list by distance and draw routes.
- **How:** processed on the device. It is sent **only** to the Google Places API (station search) and to Apple (MKDirections, route calculation), which act as processors for those functions.
- **Legal basis:** consent (you authorize it in iOS and can revoke it at any time in Settings → Privacy → Location).
- The app **does not** store location history on any developer server.

### 1.2 Usage and diagnostic data (analytics)
- **What:** navigation events (screens visited, time on each screen, taps on buttons such as search, start route, open station), device model and iOS version, approximate region (from IP), and a **pseudonymous app identifier** generated by Firebase. We also collect **crash** reports to fix errors.
- **Why:** to understand how the app is used, prioritize improvements and fix problems. We do **not** use this for advertising and do **not** collect the advertising identifier (IDFA).
- **Who processes it:** **Firebase (Google LLC)** — Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance. Data is handled under Google's policy.
- **Legal basis:** legitimate interest in improving the service; data is pseudonymized and aggregated.
- **Notifications:** if you opt in to notifications, a Firebase Messaging token is used only to deliver them.

### 1.3 In-app purchases (optional contributions)
- The app offers **one-time contributions** ("tips") to support the project. They do not unlock features.
- **Payment is processed by Apple** (App Store). The developer receives only the transaction confirmation — **never** your card number or financial data.

### 1.4 Data the app does NOT collect
Name, email, phone, contacts, photos, card data and the advertising identifier (IDFA). There is no login or account.

## 3. Public data displayed by the app

The app reproduces **public and official** databases from the ANP (inspection, retailer registry and prices), from **state Procons** and the national **SINDEC/Senacon** system (cited companies and substantiated complaints), from **IPEM-SP** (certified pumps) and content from Google (reviews, ratings), always with source and date.

- **Company** data (CNPJ, corporate name) is shown as it appears in the public databases.
- **Individual taxpayer IDs (CPF)** of sole-proprietor retailers, when present in the databases, are **masked** by the app and the data pipeline — the full document is not redistributed.
- The consumer-protection databases contain companies (legal entities), collected from transparency portals and open data (Freedom of Information Act).
- The reviews shown are authored by Google users and remain the responsibility of their authors and the source platform.

## 4. Your rights (Art. 18 of the LGPD)

You may request confirmation of processing, access, correction or deletion of data through our contact channel: **[fontinelles.com/contact](https://fontinelles.com/contact)**. In practice: **uninstalling the app deletes the data you created on the device**; the location permission can be revoked in iOS Settings; and you can disable iOS ad personalization/tracking at any time.

If you are a **subject of data present in the public databases** shown (e.g., a sole-proprietor retailer), you may contact us to review the display — and, as for the source databases, exercise your rights with the ANP, the Procon or the IPEM.

## 5. Children and adolescents

The app is intended for licensed drivers and is not directed at minors.
